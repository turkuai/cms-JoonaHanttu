Jeff the Landshark

He is a humorous and quirky Marvel Comics character. He is a magical landshark who became a fan-favorite for his adorable yet chaotic nature. First appearing in The Unbeatable Squirrel Girl, Jeff is often seen as a sidekick and is known for his love of snacks and his ability to create mayhem. Despite his cute appearance, he has a ferocious side and is loyal to those he cares about.

Magik (Illyana Rasputin)

She is a fictional superhero in Marvel Comics, and the younger sister of Colossus. She is a mutant with the ability to teleport and wield the Soulsword, a powerful weapon. Magik has often been a member of the X-Men and New Mutants, and her powers are linked to the mystical dimension known as Limbo. Her backstory includes being kidnapped to Limbo at a young age, where she trained to become a sorceress.
